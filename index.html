How do restaurants make rice taste so good?

By using good quality rice.They measure out the correct amount of water for the rice.They may season the rice with some salt

They may add aromatics. For example pandan leaf (which contains some of aromatic compounds naturally also present in rice) - making the rice smell more like rice. In an Indian restaurant setting you may be served rice that’s been cooked with some clove and/or cardamom.
